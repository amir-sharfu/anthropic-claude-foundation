# What is Claude?

## ELI5 (Explain Like I'm 5)

Imagine you have a really smart friend who has read millions of books, articles, and websites. You can ask this friend anything — and they'll write back a helpful answer in plain language.

That's Claude. It's a computer program (called an AI) made by a company called **Anthropic**. You talk to it by typing, and it talks back by typing.

---

## The Simple Version

```
You type something  →  Claude reads it  →  Claude types back
```

That's it. No magic. No thinking robot. Just a very well-trained program that is really good at understanding and generating language.

---

## What can Claude do?

| Task | Example |
|------|---------|
| Writing | "Write me a birthday message for my friend" |
| Coding | "Fix this bug in my Python code" |
| Math | "Help me solve this equation" |
| Summarizing | "Read this long article and give me the key points" |
| Answering | "What is the capital of France?" |
| Brainstorming | "Give me 10 ideas for my startup" |

---

## What Claude is NOT

- **Not a search engine** — Claude doesn't browse the internet (unless given a tool to do so)
- **Not a database** — it doesn't store your past conversations by default
- **Not always right** — it can make mistakes, always double-check important facts

---

## Who made Claude?

**Anthropic** — a safety-focused AI company. Their goal is to build AI that is:
- **Helpful** — actually useful
- **Harmless** — won't help with dangerous things
- **Honest** — won't make things up on purpose

---

## One sentence summary

> Claude is a helpful AI assistant you talk to by typing — like texting a very smart, very well-read friend.
